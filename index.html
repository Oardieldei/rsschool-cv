Sir Oardieldei

The Dark Knight

My name is Sir Oardieldei. I am the kingdom's first knight. My primary goal is the peace and protection of all the inhabitants of my homeland. I have endured much to ensure the safety of the queen and the common people. I am not proud of many of my actions, but they were necessary and made me who I am.

I started from the very bottom, a simple soldier. Unintentionally faced with a terrible choice, I accepted a curse. It gave me strength and stripped me of my humanity. With its help, I defeated countless enemies and saved tens of thousands of lives. And now my greatest desire is to become even stronger and continue to protect this world from evil.

I serve the Queen and the common people. To make our country safer for everyone, I must learn to fight on another battlefield: the internet. To do this, I need new skills. I know that the blessing of the goddess RSSchool alone will not be enough. And I am ready to work tirelessly to gain new strength, so that others can live peacefully and happily.
